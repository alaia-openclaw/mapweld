## Decision logic

- Go:
  - 6 of 7 KPI rows pass; and
  - no unresolved P0 blockers.
- Conditional go:
  - 4-5 KPI rows pass; and
  - clear remediation plan with 2-week checkpoint.
- No-go:
  - 3 or fewer KPI rows pass; or
  - unresolved trust/data durability blocker remains.

## Qualitative confidence checks

- Can teams complete activation without live handholding?
- Are objections becoming easier to resolve over time?
- Are outcomes repeatable across more than one company type?
