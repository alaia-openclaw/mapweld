# MapWeld GTM – Mission brief (from Theo)

Date: 2026-03-12

## Product
MapWeld: tablet-friendly software to load piping/fabrication PDFs/isometrics and **interactively mark** weld positions, joints, parts (spools, bolted joints, supports), capturing attributes like:
- fitter / welder
- weld ID
- WPS
- NDT / hold points
- progress status

## Pain it solves
Current workflow: people mark drawings manually → later re-enter into clunky systems / spreadsheets → bottleneck for high-volume piping & data books.

## Requested outputs
1) Competitor landscape
2) Market segmentation + who buys
3) Target client list with contacts
4) Pricing recommendations
5) Marketing plan: channels, messaging
6) Automation: recurring jobs that generate content + draft outreach

Guardrails: no outbound messages without approval.
