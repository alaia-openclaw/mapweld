# Competitors – initial short-list (weld mapping / weld tracking / fabrication traceability)

This is an **initial** list from targeted web discovery. Next iteration will expand and normalize into a matrix (features/pricing/ICP).

## Direct / close-adjacent
- WeldTrace – https://www.weldtrace.com/  
  Cloud welding management + weld mapping + mobile apps, QR labels, NDE, turnover packs.
- WeldNote – https://weldnote.com/  
  Welding book/data book generation; includes weld maps/isometrics attachments.
- WeldEye (WeldIndustry) – https://weldindustry.com/manage-your-weld-project-digitally/  
  Mobile reporting of weld progress + welding management.
- Smart Welding Manager – https://www.smartweldingmanager.com/features (referenced in AWS forum thread)  
  Weld mapping + drawing annotation + tablet.
- Weldmap Pro – https://weldmappro.com/  
  Online weld mapping for ISO PDF drawings (QC inspectors).
- Oncore “Weldy” – http://www.oncore.co.kr/eng/weldy/  
  Extracts weld joint info from ISO drawings; supports weld mapping.

## Broader fabrication / spool tracking suites (may compete on outcome)
- PipeCloud – https://pipecloud.fi/  
  Pipe prefabrication software with digital traceability & QA checkpoints.
- MSUITE FAB – https://www.msuite.com/the-future-of-pipe-fabrication/  
  Spool progress tracking by station/welder; reduces redraw/labeling.
- AUTOSPOOL (PARAS CADD) – https://parascadd.com/products/epcproman-suite-epc-project-management-control-software/autospool-piping-spooling-software/
  Spooling + joint history report for weld progress tracking.
- Stratus – https://www.stratus.build/stratus  
  Digital spooling workflow, labels, progress.
- W5 Piping / Spool Tracker – https://www.w5piping.com/
- SpoolFab – https://www.spoolfab.com/
- SPOOLCAD – https://spoolcad.com/product/
