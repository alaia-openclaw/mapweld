# Competitor matrix (v0.1) — MapWeld

Date: 2026-03-13

Focus: tools that compete with MapWeld’s promise: **tablet-first marking on PDFs/isometrics + weld/joint tracking + export for data book/turnover**.

Legend:
- ✅ = strong fit
- ◐ = partial / adjacent
- ❌ = not focus

## Immediate MapWeld differentiation thesis
1) **Fastest PDF/isometric weld marking on a tablet** (minutes, not hours)
2) **No retyping**: structured weld/joint attributes captured at source
3) **Export-ready**: data book/turnover pack outputs without admin hell
4) **Low training**: usable by yard technicians (not just QC engineers)
