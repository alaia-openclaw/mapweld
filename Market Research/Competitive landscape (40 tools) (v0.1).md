# MapWeld – Competitive landscape (40 tools) (v0.1)

Date: 2026-03-13

This is a categorized landscape of ~40 tools that compete directly or indirectly with MapWeld’s value: **tablet-friendly weld/joint/spool tracking on drawings + audit-ready turnover/data book outputs**.

> Notes:
> - Category A includes both direct weld-mapping platforms and “PDF markup” substitutes (Bluebeam/Drawboard/etc.).
> - Categories B–D are outcome competitors (spool tracking, welding traceability platforms, BIM-to-fab suites) that can displace MapWeld in deals.

---

## (A) Direct PDF / isometric weld mapping + tablet annotation

1) **Weldmap Pro** — https://weldmappro.com/
- What: Online weld mapping on ISO drawing PDFs.
- Buyer: QC inspector / welding QC lead.
- Pricing signal: positioned as free.

2) **Smart Welding Manager** — https://www.smartweldingmanager.com/
- What: Weld mapping + drawing annotation + tablet use (public references).
- Buyer: Welding QA/QC manager.

3) **WeldTrace (pictorial weld mapping)** — https://www.weldtrace.com/
- What: Cloud welding management incl. upload drawings + pictorial weld mapping.
- Buyer: Fabrication QC / project QA.

4) **Bluebeam Revu** — https://www.bluebeam.com/
- What: PDF markup/collaboration widely used for isometric markups.
- Buyer: Field engineers / QA/QC.

5) **Drawboard PDF** — https://www.drawboard.com/
- What: Tablet-first PDF markup.
- Buyer: Field supervisors.

6) **Adobe Acrobat** — https://www.adobe.com/acrobat.html
- What: PDF editor/markup + forms/signatures.
- Buyer: Doc control / QA.

7) **Foxit PDF Editor** — https://www.foxit.com/pdf-editor/
- What: PDF editor/markup alternative.

8) **Autodesk Build / PlanGrid-style drawing workflows** — https://construction.autodesk.com/
- What: Controlled drawing sets + field markups + closeout workflows.

9) **Procore (Drawings/QA)** — https://www.procore.com/
- What: Construction platform w/ drawings, markups, inspections.

10) **Fieldwire** — https://www.fieldwire.com/
- What: Field coordination app with plans/markups/tasks.
