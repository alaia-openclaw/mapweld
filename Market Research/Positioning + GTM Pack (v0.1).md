# MapWeld – Positioning + GTM Pack (v0.1)

Date: 2026-03-13

This document consolidates: positioning, differentiation, landing page outline, message pillars, content ideas, outreach sequences (draft-only), and a 30‑day GTM plan.

---

## 1) Positioning (pipe spools + offshore modules)

**One-liner**
MapWeld is the **production-grade weld + spool traceability layer** that turns shop-floor reality (WPS, welds, NDT, heat numbers, fit-up, repair loops) into **client-ready MDR/turnover packs** without spreadsheet/paper chaos.

**Who it’s for**
- Pipe spool fabricators shipping to EPCs / operators
- Offshore module yards managing multiple spools, welders, inspectors, and hold points
- QA/QC managers who own the ITP, turnover packs, and “missing paperwork” pain

**The promise**
- **Fewer NCRs, faster turnover packs, less rework** by making traceability **live**, not “end-of-job archaeology.”

**Core differentiation (plain)**
- MapWeld is **workflow + evidence + pack generation** — not a tracker, not just storage, not a generic doc system.

---

## 2) Competitive differentiation (explicit)

### vs WeldTrace
- MapWeld is **spool-first** (spools/isos/joints/modules), not just weld rows.
- **Pack automation** + completeness checks.
- **ITP hold-point workflow** (can’t proceed until cleared).
- **Repair lineage** (tracks cut-outs/repairs/retest as a chain).

### vs PipeCloud
- MapWeld goes deeper on **QC evidence + compliance** (WPS/welder continuity/consumables/MTR links/PMI).
- Built for **inspector capture** at joint level.
- Emphasis on **audit-ready chain of evidence**.

### vs Stratus
- Not generic doc control. **Built for welding reality**.
- Structured entities (weld/joint/spool) produce documents as outputs.
- Faster adoption for shop-floor users.

### vs lightweight PDF tools (folders + fillable PDFs)
- Completeness gates prevent “missing page 17” at handover.
- “Search & trace in seconds” by weld/joint.
- Revision/change control that survives rework.

## 3) Message pillars

### Pillar 1 — Turnover packs without fire drills
- Auto-assemble MDR/turnover packs from verified weld/joint evidence
- Pack readiness visibility by spool/module/system

### Pillar 2 — Traceability you can defend (audit-grade)
- Weld → joint → spool → iso → system → module chain
- NDT/PWHT/PMI/VT attachments tied to correct weld revision
- Repair loops tracked as lineage

### Pillar 3 — Shop-floor adoption that doesn’t collapse
- Fast capture for welders/QC/inspectors (tablet)
- ITP hold points + signoffs
- Works alongside existing ERP/doc control

## 4) Landing page outline (single page)

**Hero**
- Headline: “Live weld + spool traceability for pipe fabrication and offshore modules.”
- Subhead: “Capture evidence at the weld and generate audit-ready turnover packs.”
- CTA: Book a 20‑min demo
- Secondary: Download sample turnover pack output

**Pain**
- “Excel says complete. Client says missing.”
- “Repairs break traceability.”
- “Docs live in five places.”

**What it does**
- Spool/joint/weld register from isos
- WPS assignment + compliance capture
- Welder assignment + continuity/qual tracking
- ITP hold points + signoffs
- NDT/PWHT/PMI/VT evidence capture
- Repair loop tracking
- Pack generation + completeness checks

**How it works**
1) Import isos / build spool structure
2) Capture evidence as work happens
3) Generate packs + hand over

**Why it wins**
- Comparison blocks vs WeldTrace / PipeCloud / Stratus / PDF-only

**Outputs**
- Weld record, spool dashboard, pack readiness, repair lineage

**Implementation**
- 1–2 week pilot, scale by system
- Exports: Excel + PDF packs; API later
