# Competitor deep-dive — WeldEye (Kemppi)

URL: https://www.weldeye.com/

## What they sell (positioning)
- Cloud-based **welding production management** platform with modular add-ons.
- Leads with **100% traceability**, standards compliance (ISO/ASME/AWS), and parameter collection.

## Modules / capabilities (from public pages)
Quality Management page highlights:
- Collect welding parameters (voltage/current/wire feed), **heat input** calculations, deviation alerts.
- “Project documentation can be printed… with a few clicks.”
- Mobile app + web access; “compatible with all welding equipment brands”; integrations possible.

## Pricing signals (public)
Source: https://www.weldeye.com/pricing

## How they win (hypothesis)
- When a buyer cares about **parameter-based compliance** (not just inspections + docs).
- When they already use (or are sold on) a Kemppi ecosystem / shopfloor digitization.

## MapWeld implications
- WeldEye is *welding-process-data-first*.
- MapWeld wedge stays: **fast ISO/PDF weld mapping + evidence capture + turnover readiness**, and export into existing QA systems.
- Useful positioning line: “We don’t need to connect to your welders to remove the turnover-pack bottleneck.”
