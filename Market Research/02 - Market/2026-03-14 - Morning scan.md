## Quick takeaways for MapWeld GTM

- The **output-side promise (turnover/data book packaging)** is a clear wedge (Codeware explicitly markets this). MapWeld should keep “turnover-ready exports” front-and-center.
- Two poles in the market:
  - **Heavy welding/compliance suites** (Kemppi WeldEye, Fronius WeldCube, Cleopatra)
  - **Pipe/spool execution platforms** (PipeCloud, Paras CADD, SmartPlant Spoolgen)
  MapWeld can sit between them as the **fastest PDF/isometric-first capture layer**.
- Integration story worth exploring: “Export clean weld/joint data → feed WeldEye/WeldCube/turnover pack tools” vs trying to replicate full QA ecosystems.
