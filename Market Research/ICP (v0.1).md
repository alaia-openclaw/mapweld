# ICP & Positioning (v0.1)

## Primary buyer personas
- QA/QC Manager (fabrication yard)
- Project Manager / Construction Manager
- Document Control / Turnover lead (data book pain)
- Welding coordinator

## Primary segments
- Pipe spool fabricators (onshore & offshore modules)
- EPC subcontractors with high weld volumes
- Maintenance shutdown contractors (time pressure + documentation)

## Core positioning
**“Turn PDFs into a live weld map on a tablet — in minutes — and generate the data book without retyping.”**

## Proof points to collect
- Time saved per isometric
- Error reduction (missing weld IDs / wrong welder)
- Faster turnover pack generation
